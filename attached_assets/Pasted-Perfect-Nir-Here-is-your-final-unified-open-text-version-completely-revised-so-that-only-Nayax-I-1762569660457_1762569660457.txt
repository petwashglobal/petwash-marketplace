Legal Head Company: Pet Wash Ltd (פט וואש בע״מ)
All intellectual property, brands, domains, data systems, hardware designs, software, and merchant accounts are owned and controlled solely by Pet Wash Ltd.
All revenues, contracts, and user payments flow through Pet Wash Ltd.
No other company, subsidiary, or individual has the right to receive or process payments outside of Pet Wash Ltd.

Exclusive Payment Processor:
All transactions across every platform, service, and partner channel are processed only via Nayax Israel.
This includes kiosks, digital wallets, loyalty programs, mobile apps, and online bookings.
Nayax Israel is the single authorized gateway for credit cards, debit cards, QR payments, Apple Pay, and Google Pay integrations.
All settlements are received directly into Pet Wash Ltd’s designated merchant bank account.
Partners, franchisees, or contractors are never permitted to use personal or third-party merchant accounts.

5. Financial Operations

5.1 Payments and Receipts
	•	Every customer transaction is processed exclusively through Nayax Israel.
	•	Each platform connects directly to Pet Wash Ltd’s Nayax merchant account via certified API.
	•	Receipts & Invoices: automatically generated by Pet Wash Ltd and issued to customers in real time.
	•	Refund Policy: No refunds for completed wash sessions. Credit only if system-verified malfunction occurred.
	•	Settlement: Nayax Israel deposits daily or weekly to Pet Wash Ltd bank account.
	•	Currency: Israeli Shekel (ILS) for domestic; other currencies converted by Nayax per current rate before settlement.

5.2 Accounting and Audit
	•	Centralized accounting using Xero or QuickBooks with cost centers per platform.
	•	Automated reconciliation between Nayax reports, accounting ledger, and bank feed.
	•	Monthly closing by CFO, quarterly audit by external accountant.
	•	Annual statutory audit under Israeli law.

5.3 Expense and Procurement
	•	All purchases approved through digital PO system.
	•	Three-quote rule for any new vendor.
	•	Pet Wash Ltd owns all physical equipment and consumables inventory; partners buy only via authorized portal.

5.4 Tax and Compliance
	•	VAT and income tax filed under Pet Wash Ltd.
	•	Withholding applied to contractors per Israeli regulation.
	•	AI-based tax monitor tracks filing dates, payment thresholds, and alerts CFO of any deviation.
