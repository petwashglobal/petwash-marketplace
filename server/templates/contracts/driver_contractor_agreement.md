## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 1.1 Transport Services
The Contractor agrees to provide independent pet transport services ("Services") including:
- Pet pickup from client locations
- Safe transport to destination (veterinary clinics, grooming facilities, boarding, airports, etc.)
- Pet delivery to designated recipients
- Route optimization and timely completion
- Vehicle cleanliness and maintenance

### 1.2 Service Standards
The Contractor shall:
- Maintain professional conduct at all times
- Follow all PetTrek™ safety protocols
- Secure pets properly during transport (crates, harnesses, barriers)
- Monitor pet wellbeing throughout journey
- Communicate proactively with clients and Pet Wash™ dispatch
- Complete delivery confirmations with photo documentation
- Report incidents immediately

### 1.3 Independent Contractor Status
The Contractor is an independent contractor, not an employee. The Contractor:
- Controls how, when, and where Services are performed
- Provides own vehicle, equipment, and supplies
- Bears all vehicle operating costs (fuel, maintenance, insurance, registration)
- Pays own taxes and contributions
- Is free to accept or decline service requests

## 4. SAFETY & LIABILITY

### 4.1 Pet Safety Protocols
The Contractor shall:
- Inspect pet health before transport (refuse sick/injured pets without veterinary clearance)
- Use appropriate restraints for all pets
- Never leave pets unattended in vehicle
- Maintain comfortable temperature at all times
- Carry emergency pet first aid kit
- Know locations of emergency veterinary clinics along routes

### 4.2 Incident Reporting
Any incident must be reported within {{incident_reporting_hours}} hours:
- Pet injury or illness during transport
- Vehicle accidents or breakdowns
- Client complaints or disputes
- Lost or escaped pets
- Property damage

### 4.3 Liability & Indemnification
**Company Liability:** Pet Wash™ provides up to {{company_liability_limit}} liability coverage per incident.

**Contractor Liability:** The Contractor indemnifies Pet Wash™ against:
